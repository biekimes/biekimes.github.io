About Me

My name is Bum. Weird name, I know. I've embodied my weirdness with a Diogenic spirit and an intent to live the life I want.

After working in translating, advertising, and education, I started learning programming to obtain a skill I can build upon and one that I can take anywhere in the world.

I started by learning the usual: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on my own through online resources. Then I attended an offline bootcamp at a coding school in Seoul to learn Front-End Web Development and Web Design. There, I learned JS libraries and frameworks like JQuery, React, and BootStrap. I learned how to set up environments like Node.js, as well as becoming familiar with design tools like Figma and Kakao Oven.

You can check out my projects to see what I can do, but like most things in life, this is a continuous learning process and I will add more to my skills and experience as time goes on.
