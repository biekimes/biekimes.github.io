Dancing on a Mountaintop

The sun glares down and sweat glistens on our backs. The view is worth it.

The perfect place for a dance.

Another peak, another view. I await more with you.

Commit to your silliness. Unapologetically you.

Sliding down the jungle. I treasure the feeling of pebbles in my shoes.

The best view for a puska pissalla.

You added sparks to my life.
