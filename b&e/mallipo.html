Chicken & Beer

We missed the sunset this day. Did we almost miss each other?

You peak out like the sun peaks out through the clouds. Even a glimpse is blinding.

Pebbles crunch under feet. Their colors rub against each other to murmur what we were holding.

The Cosmic Artist splashes color unto the sky. A show for us to take in.

I wanted to whisper into the winds a message you might hear across the globe.

Chicken and Beer and waves made us more honest. Yet I couldn't reveal all that I held.

Was this a beacon towards a new direction?
